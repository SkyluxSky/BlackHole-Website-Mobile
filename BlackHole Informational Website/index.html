Black Hole in M87 Galaxy

First time, mankind captured a picture of the Black Hole

At 10 April, 2019 - 13:00 UTC Event Horizon Telescope Astronomers released the first picture of the black hole.
This black hole is in the center of the of the M87 Galaxy and is 55 Million Light Years away from us.
These image's captured by the Event Horizon Telescope were taken from six different locations on Earth.

What Is A Black Hole?

Black Hole is a region of spacetime exhibiting such strong gravitational effects that nothing not even particles and electromagnetic radiation such as light can escape from inside it.
